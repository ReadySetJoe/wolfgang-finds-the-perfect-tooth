


Wolfgang Finds the Perfect Tooth

A musical performed by Wolfgang Wallace
Script, music, and lyrics by Matthew C. Wallace
Edited by Joseph Powers
Wolfgang Finds the Perfect Tooth: A Live-Band Musical Adventure

Cast
THE PATIENT: A nervous and reluctant hero with a hidden streak of courage. 
THE DENTIST: A charming but sinister figure obsessed with power and perfection. 
WOLFMAN: A vengeful creature with a bone to pick (literally) and a love for chaos. 
THE COLONEL: An eccentric military man seeking redemption, who only speaks in rhymes. 
ENSEMBLE: Various characters, including the HAG, HYGIENIST, DEMON, and RECEPTIONIST.  
Color Key for Lyrics
THE PATIENT - Black
THE DENTIST - Blue
WOLFMAN - Magenta
THE COLONEL - Orange
THE HYGIENIST - Red

Setting
The story unfolds across a dentist office, dentist’s waiting room, Hell, and Court of Hell. The time period is modern day, in a world with magic and mythological figures, where the moon has been destroyed to defeat an uprising of Wolfmen.

ACT 2, SCENE 1: The Dentist’s Office Confrontation
The scene opens inside the surgery room. The DENTIST is holding the Perfect Tooth with glee, examining it like a prized treasure. The PATIENT is still unconscious in the chair, snoring softly, while the Dental HYGIENIST stands nervously by the tray of instruments.
DENTIST
(to himself, admiring the tooth)
Finally, After all these years, the key to everything... right in my hands.
(grinning madly)
With this, the world will kneel before me... and she will be mine.
HYGIENIST
(glancing nervously toward the door)
Doctor, are you sure this is a good idea? What if someone finds out? I mean, we... we did just take it from him.
DENTIST
(waving her off, still focused on the tooth)
Bah! He won’t miss it. It would simply go to waste in such a pedestrian mouth. This… This is my destiny!
[The door to the surgery room suddenly swings open, and the WOLFMAN bursts in, followed closely by the COLONEL. The WOLFMAN’s eyes immediately lock onto the tooth in the Dentist’s hand.]
WOLFMAN
(growling, pointing)
There it is! The Perfect Tooth!
The COLONEL
Congratulations Doctor, you’ve found a nice prize,
I suggest you hand it over, lest my friend gouge out your eyes
[The DENTIST stiffens, clutching the tooth protectively. The HYGIENIST steps back, her face pale with fear.]
DENTIST
(attempting to lie) Who? Me? Tooth? Perfect? Absolute power and world domination? Why I have no idea what you’re talking about. Might I interest you fine fellows in some nitrous oxide? First hit’s free!
WOLFMAN
(taking a threatening step forward)
Don’t bullshit me Dentist, that’s the Perfect Tooth. Give it to me, or I’ll tear you apart!
HYGIENIST
(panicking)
Doctor, maybe we should—
[The WOLFMAN’s ears perk up at this, and he grins, sensing weakness.]
WOLFMAN
Oh? She knows something, doesn’t she?
(turning to the HYGIENIST, his tone menacing)
Go on, tell me what you know.
[The HYGIENIST hesitates, glancing nervously between the DENTIST and the WOLFMAN. Finally, under the pressure, she speaks.]
HYGIENIST
(stammering)
It… it’s true. The Doctor found the Perfect Tooth, and... and he was going to use it to summon… her. He thinks she’ll grant him world domination or something, but... she’s evil! I don’t wanna see the Doctor get hurt!

(nodding solemnly)
A dangerous path, we know it’s true,
But still the wish calls out to you.
WOLFMAN
(grinning wolfishly)

(begins laughing menacingly, holding up the tooth defiantly)
You fools. You think mere threats will separate me from my glorious purpose? If you want it… (brandishing a scalpel), come and get it.
[As the tension builds, the PATIENT begins to stir in the dentist’s chair. He groans softly, his hand reaching up to his mouth. His eyes open, groggy and confused.]
PATIENT
(slurred, still half-asleep)
Wh—what... what happened...?
[The PATIENT sits up slowly, blinking as he tries to regain his senses. He touches his jaw, wincing, then suddenly freezes.]
PATIENT
(panicking)
My tooth! Where’s my tooth?! What did you do to my tooth!?
[He leaps out of the chair, pointing accusingly at the DENTIST.]
PATIENT
You stole my tooth! That was my favorite one! I want it back!
[The DENTIST scoffs, still holding the Perfect Tooth above his head.]
DENTIST
(mocking)
Oh, this little thing? Don’t be ridiculous. You don’t even know what you had, ______. Better in my hands than in your worthless mouth.
[The PATIENT, still in shock and anger, looks around the room at the strange gathering.]
PATIENT
(furious)
That’s my tooth, and I’m going to take it back!
[The PATIENT charges at the DENTIST, but before he can reach him, the DENTIST swiftly sidesteps and pins him against the wall.]
DENTIST
(grinning maliciously)
Oh no, no, no. You don’t understand, do you? I’ve waited too long for this moment. You’re not taking anything from me. None of you are.
[As the tension between the DENTIST, the PATIENT, and the WOLFMAN rises, the WOLFMAN suddenly lunges forward. He grabs the DENTIST by the throat, lifting him off the ground. The DENTIST gasps, dropping the Perfect Tooth, which rolls under a nearby chair. Before the PATIENT can react, the WOLFMAN swings his other arm, slamming him into the wall, knocking the wind out of him.]
WOLFMAN
(puffing his chest confidently)
Not a chance, pipsqueek. The tooth is mine, and none of you weaklings are gonna stand in my way.
[The PATIENT crawls towards a backpack from the other side of the room but the WOLFMAN steps on his back, pinning him to the floor. The DENTIST struggles in the WOLFMAN’s grip, but he’s powerless against the WOLFMAN’s brute strength.]
WOLFMAN

(breaking free from Wolfman’s grasp, he pulls the pin from the grenade, gasping for breath)

WOLFMAN

PATIENT

HYGENIST
